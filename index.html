New Matters is a multidisciplinary experiential performance that will take visitors on a journey of exploring sensations, feelings and emotions. Visual arts, dance, color, sound, smell, and taste – everything will be joint together for the visitor to experience. Sensitivity, individual perception and neurodiversity are at the heart of the project.
